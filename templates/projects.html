What we offer

Would your project or organisation benefit from our technical expertise? We're here to help. Our community of volunteer designers, developers and general technologists are eager to get to work!

We aim to provide radical organisations with hands-on technical support. We work with selected projects to understand their needs and constraints, design an initial approach to the problem and find them the (volunteer) developers and designers that can help make their projects a success.

We accept projects on a rolling basis. Apply by filling out our form.

Apply for support

Types of projects we accept

Radical Engineers supports organisations and projects with radical aims. We believe that we cannot afford to merely fight symptoms, but have to instead radically rethink the economic and social systems that produce them.

All sizes are welcome We support organisations of all sizes, experience levels and types (e.g. NGO, charities, corporations).

We are London-based, but you don’t have to be. If your organisation supports remote work, then so do we!

If you’re not sure you qualify, or have other questions, drop us a line - info@radicalengineers.com
